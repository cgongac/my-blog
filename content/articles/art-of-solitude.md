## The Value of Silence

In a world that never stops talking, finding silence is an act of rebellion. Solitude is potential; it is the soil in which the seeds of creativity are sown. 

When we are alone, we are free from the gaze of others. This freedom allows us to explore our thoughts without censorship or performance.

I've been trying to spend at least one hour a day in complete silence. No music, no podcasts, no books. Just me and my thoughts (and sometimes a notebook). It was uncomfortable at first, almost painful. But slowly, the noise in my head settled, and clarity emerged.
